What is the definition of the word Artisan and what exactly is Artisan bread?
The definition of Artisan is A person skilled in making a product by hand. A Person that has a passion and works hard to get the full experience of his/her trade.
So what are Artisan bread then?
Artisan bread uses traditional methods, only uses natural ingredients, takes longer to make and is made by an artisan.

Who is our Artisan Bakers?

Johan Germishuys.
Also known as Johannes Jacobus Germishuys. He is the Artisan that provides freshly baked goods to the businesses and restaurants of Upington. A hard-working person. a Baker that has been in the Baking business for years, now an Artisan and known for baking the best Sourgdougs and Ciabattas in Upington. Johan is also known for being friendly to the community. Always ensuring a heartwarming environment when entering the bakery and leaving with a smile after a good chat with a freshly baked product. Johan managed to learn the Artisan trade when he was venturing as a Farmer in North America, Arkansas. On his adventure, he met various famous artisan bakers. Studying the trade and learning from the best bakers in America to Yzerfontein in South Africa. Showing Johan the ropes of mastering the trade of an Artisan. He is not the only baker in the business, Johan is accompanied by Magdelene also a Baker. Magdelene mastered her Artisan trade with the help of Johan's assistance and is now popular among the most bakers in Upington.

Johan isn't just a baker. He is a hard-working man that is pure to his family and his community! - Chris Germishuys
